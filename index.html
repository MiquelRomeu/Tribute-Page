Ferran Adrià

Ferran Adrià, head chef of the spanish restaurant elBulli, one of the best chefs in the world.

Here's a time line of Ferran Adria's life:

  • 1980: Ferran Adrià began his culinary career as a dishwasher, where he learned traditional spanish cuisine.
  • 1981: At 19 he was drafted to military service, where he worked as a cook.
  • 1984: At the age of 22, Ferran joined the kitchen staff of elBulli as a line cook.
  • 1985: He beacame the head chef.
  • 1990-1999: Ferran Adrià explores new ways of cooking, and starts a culinary revolution.
  • 1999: An spanish newspaper makes a reportage called "Ferran Adrià the best chef in the world.
  • 2004: The New York Times writes about him and catalog him as one of the top 100 most influencing people in the world.
  • 2006: elBulli is elected the best restaurant in the world for the second time.
  • 2009: elBulli wins the best restaurant price for the fifth time.
